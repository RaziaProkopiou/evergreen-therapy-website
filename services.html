Our Services

At Evergreen Family Therapy Centre, we offer a range of professional services designed to support individuals, families, and communities in navigating life’s challenges. Our goal is to provide clear, effective, and compassionate guidance in a safe and supportive environment.

Individual Therapy

Life’s challenges can sometimes feel overwhelming, but you don’t have to navigate them alone. Our individual therapy sessions provide a safe and supportive space for self-reflection, personal growth, and emotional well-being. We help individuals:

  • Understand and process their thoughts and feelings
  • Develop healthy coping strategies for managing stress and life’s demands
  • Navigate personal struggles and major life changes
  • Build confidence and a stronger sense of self

Each session is tailored to your unique needs, offering guidance, clarity, and support in a way that feels right for you.

Family Therapy & Mediation

Families go through many changes and challenges that can sometimes create tension or misunderstandings. Our family therapy and mediation services provide a safe and supportive space to help families:

  • Improve communication and strengthen connections
  • Work through conflicts in a respectful and constructive way
  • Develop parenting approaches that foster positive family dynamics
  • Create parenting plans that prioritize children’s well-being
  • Navigate transitions such as separation or co-parenting with guidance and support

We offer a neutral and compassionate environment where families can work through challenges together and build stronger, more supportive relationships.

Group Therapy

Sometimes, healing happens best in a community. Our group therapy sessions provide a supportive space where individuals can connect with others who share similar experiences. These sessions help participants:

  • Share their challenges in a judgment-free environment
  • Gain new perspectives and insights from others
  • Build a strong support network
  • Learn effective coping skills through guided discussions

Group therapy is available for various topics, including stress management, parenting support, and emotional well-being.

Case Management & Resource Coordination

Navigating social services and community resources can be confusing. Our case management services help individuals and families:

  • Access the right resources for their specific needs
  • Coordinate with government and NGO services
  • Advocacy and social justice
  • Develop a plan to address long-term challenges

We take a hands-on approach to ensure that no one is left without the support they need.

Crisis Intervention

When unexpected challenges arise, immediate support is essential. Our crisis intervention services provide:

  • Immediate emotional support for individuals in distress
  • Practical guidance during difficult life events
  • Safety planning and risk assessment for those in vulnerable situations
  • Emergency referrals to additional professional services if needed

We are committed to helping individuals and families navigate crises with care, professionalism, and urgency.

Get the Support You Need

At Evergreen Family Therapy Centre, we believe that everyone deserves access to professional guidance and support. Whether you’re seeking personal growth, family healing, or crisis intervention, we’re here to help.

Contact us today to learn more about our services or to schedule an appointment.
