About Evergreen Family Therapy Centre

Compassionate Support for Families, By Families

Welcome to Evergreen Family Therapy Centre, where healing, guidance, and empowerment come together to support individuals and families navigating life’s challenges.

About the Founder

I am a registered Social Worker with 16 years of experience in both Government and the NGO sector. My career has taken me through various specialized fields, including:

  • Child Care and Protection
  • Children’s Court Matters
  • Family and Divorce Mediation
  • Probation Services
  • Criminal Justice Court Support

Rooted in Community

My journey in social work began in a small town on the West Coast, but I always knew I would return to the place I was born and raised—Atlantis, just outside of Cape Town. Having witnessed firsthand the lack of accessible support services, I was determined to create a space where individuals and families could find clear, direct, and professional guidance without having to seek help far from home. With a deep understanding of the complex social challenges faced by families here, I recognize the critical need for accessible support services.

The Inspiration Behind Evergreen

In recent years, I too faced personal challenges and found myself having to seek professional services and support outside of my community to address those challenges. Through my own personal experiences, I encountered the frustration of seeking professional support only to be referred from one service to another without clear guidance. I saw first-hand the gaps in available resources and the overwhelming struggles families face when trying to access help.
This journey ignited my passion to create a trusted, local resource—a place where families and individuals can find the support, guidance, and therapeutic services they need, right within their own community. Thus, Evergreen Family Therapy Centre was born.

A Commitment to Community and Care

Evergreen Family Therapy Centre was founded with a clear mission: to provide compassionate, professional, and accessible support for individuals and families navigating life’s challenges.

Our Mission (Jy kan dit aanpas)

At Evergreen Family Therapy Centre, our goal is to:

  • Empower families and individuals by providing professional therapeutic support.
  • Offer guidance through life’s most challenging moments with compassion and understanding.
  • Bridge the gap in accessible social services within the Atlantis community.

We are committed to walking this journey with you, ensuring that you feel heard, supported, and equipped to overcome life’s challenges.

Let Evergreen Family Therapy Centre be your safe space for growth and healing.
