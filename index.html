Gain Clarity, Support, and Solutions for Life’s Challenges

Evergreen Family Therapy Centre provides expert guidance to help individuals, families, and communities navigate difficulties, strengthen relationships, and access the resources they need. From therapy and mediation to crisis intervention, we offer professional, compassionate care—right in your community.

Why Evergreen?

With 16 years of experience in social work—spanning child protection, family mediation, and probation services—Evergreen Family Therapy Centre was founded to provide clear, direct, and effective guidance. Rooted in a deep understanding of the community’s unique challenges, our mission is to offer accessible, professional support close to home.

Evergreen is pioneered by Social Service Practitioners experienced in the field of Social Work in Child Care and Protection, Children’s Court matters, Mediation in Family and Divorce matters, to Probation Services and working in the Criminal Justice Courts.

What does Evergreen Offer?

A place that will to assist, guide and support You and your family with clear, direct, detailed and professional services as close to home as possible.


Our Services:

  • Individual Therapy – Helping individuals explore their thoughts, feelings, and behaviours to address personal and social challenges.
  • Family Therapy – Facilitating communication and resolving conflicts within families. Which includes mediation, parenting plans and support services to families.
  • Group Therapy – Providing a supportive environment for individuals to share experiences and learn from others.
  • Case Management – Coordinating services and resources to support individuals and families.
  • Crisis Intervention – Providing immediate support and intervention during crises.

At Evergreen Family Therapy Centre, you’ll find the care, understanding, and professional support your family needs to heal and thrive.
